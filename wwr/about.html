About us - Down the river

Down the River is a white-water rafting company dedicated to adventure, safety, and unforgettable journeys. Our purpose is to connect people with nature's power while fostering teamwork and courage. Our mission is guiding with expertise and care. Our creed is respect for river and rider.

History

Founded by passionate river guides, Down the River began as a small local outfit dedicated to sharing the thrill of white-water rafting. Over time, it grew into a trusted company known for safety, adventure, and unforgettable experiences. With decades of guiding expertise, Down the River has introduced countless adventurers to the beauty and power of rivers, building a legacy of respect for nature, teamwork, and the spirit of exploration.
