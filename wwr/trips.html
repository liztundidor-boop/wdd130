Beginner Rapids Expedition

Perfect for first-timers! Enjoy a half-day on the calm stretches of the river with a few exciting rapids. Our guides will keep you safe while you learn the basics and have fun!

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Price: $120 per person

Intermediate White-Water Challenge

Step up your adventure with this full-day experience! Tackle class III–IV rapids and enjoy a picnic lunch by the riverside. Ideal for those who’ve rafted before.

  • Duration: 1 day
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Price: $220 per person

Extreme Rapids Adventure

For thrill seekers only! Conquer some of the most intense rapids in the region. Includes camping overnight and expert guidance for the ultimate adrenaline rush.

  • Duration: 2 days
  • Difficulty: Advanced
  • Price: $350 per person
